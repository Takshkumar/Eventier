Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an event?

You can book events directly through our website by clicking the "Book Now" button on any event page. You'll be guided through a simple booking process.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the event. Please contact our support team for assistance with cancellations.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer group discounts for bookings of 10 or more people. Contact us for more information about group rates.

How can I become an event organizer?

We welcome new event organizers! Please contact us through the form above or email us at partnerships@eventier.com to learn more.
